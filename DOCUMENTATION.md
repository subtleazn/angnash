## Overview
The `angnash` theme is a custom Blogger theme. This document aims to provide clear instructions and insights into its structure, functionality, and how to maintain or extend it.

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites
*   A Blogger account.
*   Basic understanding of HTML, CSS, and Blogger's template language.

### Installation
1.  Navigate to your Blogger dashboard.
2.  Go to 'Theme' -> 'Backup/Restore'.
3.  Upload the `theme.xml` file.
